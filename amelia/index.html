Features

Everything Amelia handles for you.

Built around how executive assistants actually work — not a generic productivity app.

✦
AI Daily Brief

Morning and evening modes. Amelia surfaces what matters most — urgent emails, conflicts, free windows — so you start with clarity every single day.

📧
Smart Inbox

Emails sorted by priority. AI drafts replies in your voice — editable before sending. One-click to regenerate if the first draft isn't right.

📅
Meeting Command Centre

Full timeline with expandable panels per meeting. Agenda templates (Standard, Board, 1:1, Vendor), AI-improved, linked notes, and file uploads — all in one place.

🎙️
Live Transcription & Minutes

Record in Chrome or paste text. Amelia generates structured minutes with Summary, Key Decisions, Action Items, and Follow-ups — instantly.

📋
Project Tracking

Visual progress bars, task checklists, linked notes and files per project. Tasks sync with your daily to-do list automatically.

🗂
Archive & Reports

Archive by project or month. Amelia generates a professional summary report — ready to share with stakeholders or keep for your own records.

📝
Quick Note

Floating notepad always at hand. Save to today's log or archive with one click. Never lose a thought during a busy day.

🔗
Google Workspace Integration

Open any meeting's agenda or minutes directly in Google Docs. Quick-access to Sheets, Slides, Forms, and Drive from the dashboard.

📅
Smart Calendar

Month view with event indicators. Click any day to see the schedule, add tasks, and link to archived notes. Clock widget shows your next meeting at a glance.
