How It Works
One dashboard. Everything your day needs.

Amelia brings your email, meetings, tasks, and briefings into a single AI-powered workspace — so you always know exactly what needs your attention.

1

Start your morning

Amelia generates your morning brief — flagging urgent emails, highlighting today's meetings, and surfacing the three tasks that matter most. You walk in already prepared.
