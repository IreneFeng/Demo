How It Works
From first message to confirmed booking — in minutes.
Annie handles the entire booking flow so you never have to interrupt your work.
1

Customer reaches out

A customer visits your Annie booking link and describes their problem in natural language — just like texting a person.

2

Annie qualifies & schedules

Annie detects urgency, identifies the service needed, confirms the ZIP code is in your coverage area, collects customer info, and offers available time slots based on your real hours.

3

You see it instantly

A new booking appears on your merchant dashboard. Emergency jobs are flagged in red. Regular jobs are sorted by date and time. Complete or cancel with one click.
